Mission of Shiv Construction

Shiv Construction is a name to reckon on in the civil repair, reconstruction and fabrication industry. With our 7 years of vast experience, the company has achieved Happy clients, with an escalating growth with a trail of an ever expanding client base.


Above and beyond our site jobs (Civil & Fabrication) such as repairs & rehabilitation of buildings bridges.roads, fabrication, erection and laying of water pipe lines and associated civil/steelstructural work, our fabrication industrial unit specializes in custom-built shuttering, centering and structural manufacturing. We have proficiently utilized our expertise for the repairs and maintenance of heavy duty construction works, which we do on regular basis. Our 7 years of effective functioning has helped us attain in-depth Techno commercial knowledge in the domain of special repair works of building, bridges & roads
